display the name, sal,job n raise in the salary 
The raise is to be given under following scenarios –
Employees from deptno 20 from job Clerk should get 30% raise in their salary.
Employees from deptno 20 from job Analyst should get 50% raise in their salary.
Employees from deptno 30 from job Manager should get 60% raise in their salary.
Employees from deptno 30 from job Salesman should get 70% raise in their salary.
Employees from deptno 10 from job Clerk should get 10% raise in their salary.
The remaining employees should get a raise of 500.
SELECT 
    ename, 
    sal, 
    job, 
    deptno,
    CASE 
        WHEN deptno = 20 AND job = 'CLERK'    THEN sal * 0.30
        WHEN deptno = 20 AND job = 'ANALYST'  THEN sal * 0.50
        WHEN deptno = 30 AND job = 'MANAGER'  THEN sal * 0.60
        WHEN deptno = 30 AND job = 'SALESMAN' THEN sal * 0.70
        WHEN deptno = 10 AND job = 'CLERK'    THEN sal * 0.10
        ELSE 500 
    END AS raise
FROM emp;
